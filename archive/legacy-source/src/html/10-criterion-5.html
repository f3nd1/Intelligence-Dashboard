How to read 5.1.1

This dashboard checks whether UCC can demonstrate that each Course and Module was designed, approved, delivered with documented evidence, and periodically reviewed.

Course
UCC display term for ERPNext Program.
Module
UCC display term for ERPNext Course.
Evidence
A populated field, child table, approval, review record, or linked document that supports an EduTrust requirement.
Evidence gap
A required or expected evidence item that is empty, missing, overdue, or cannot be confirmed from an available DocType.

Status meanings

Good
The check met the configured rule for the selected scope.
Warning
Evidence exists but is incomplete, pending, or needs manual confirmation.
Risk
Evidence is missing, overdue, or falls below the configured rule.
Unavailable
The dashboard could not confirm a readable source. This is not the same as zero records.

Subpage definitions

Proposal
Checks Course Proposal rationale, learner profile, pedagogy, curriculum, assessment, risks/resources, approvals, decision dates, and SSG approval date.
Module Design
Checks Module learning outcomes, lesson plans, teaching approach, assessment criteria, and resources.
Course Review
Checks review status, review dates, recommendations, next review date, and action-plan progress.
Evidence Gaps
Combines missing or incomplete proposal, module-design, and review evidence into one follow-up list.

How to verify a result

  1. Open the relevant Q&A row.
  2. Use the DocType link in the Source column.
  3. Apply the same Course, Module, Academic Year, or date filters shown at the top.
  4. Open individual records from the dashboard tables or affected-record dialog.
  5. Confirm the exact fields listed in the source description.
