- policy/subcriterion navigation;
- source and metric readiness;
- six KPI cards;
- diagram/table views;
- management questions;

- permission-aware drill-down;
- data-quality checks;
- source registry;
- View tools and diagnostics.

## Meaning of live foundation

The dashboard calls a real API, evaluates current-user permissions, resolves
approved candidate DocTypes/fields, returns readiness, and permits safe
drill-downs. It may still report unsupported policy measures where source
metadata or business rules have not been supplied.
