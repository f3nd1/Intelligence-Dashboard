# Criterion 1 Source and Metric Map

## 1.1.1 Leadership and Corporate Governance

Live checks include governance oversight, stakeholder records and engagement,
controlled policies, overdue policy review, ESG records, business-impact
assessments, risks, management reviews and Quality Actions.

The policy describes VMVQ governance, stakeholder engagement, committee
oversight, environmental scanning, risk, sustainability and governance review.
Committee-member child-table completeness remains unsupported until the
installed child DocType definitions are supplied.

## 1.2.1 Strategic Planning

Live checks include Quality Goals, Staff Goals, Appraisals, Management Reviews,
Quality Actions, strategic risks and business-impact assessments.

The policy links long-term goals, KRAs, KPIs, departmental targets and staff
goals to Quality Goal, Staff Goal, Appraisal, Management Review and Quality
Action records.

## Safe implementation boundary

- API calls use technical DocType names only.
- Current-user read permission is required for every source.
- Zero matching records remains an available source.
- KPI achievement is not guessed when child objective rules are unavailable.
