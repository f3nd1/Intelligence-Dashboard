Management Review Instructions
The Management Review DocType is designed for conducting structured Management Reviews, integrating key QMS aspects to support strategic and operational goals.

Tab Name	Description
🎯 Tactical Strategy	Aligns governance and verifies policies, ensuring strategic and policy coherence with management review processes.
💼 Harmonizing Resources	Evaluates the adequacy of all organizational resources including financial metrics, and manages asset infrastructure to robustly support and enhance QMS operational effectiveness.
📈 Evaluating Performance	Evaluates KPIs, process adherence, audits, and external provider performance, identifying areas for QMS improvement.
🔒 Securing Compliance	Reviews nonconformance actions and risks, ensuring QMS compliance and strategic adaptability to internal and external changes.
🛡️ Implementing Resilience	Focuses on business continuity, change management and social responsibility strategies to enhance organizational resilience and QMS alignment.
💡 Sustaining Progression	Uses stakeholder feedback to identify and implement improvement opportunities, enhancing QMS effectiveness and customer satisfaction.

Utilize this DocType to guide Management Reviews, ensuring comprehensive coverage and alignment with our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Current State Baseline
Section Break
current_state_baseline_section
Establishes a factual baseline of the institution’s current operating context, performance outcomes, capabilities, constraints, and compliance status to inform evidence-based strategic planning and decision-making. (ISO 9001:2015 4.1 Organisational Context, 9.1 Performance Evaluation / GD4 1.2.1 – Strategic planning)
22	
Note
Text Editor
note_current_state
Current State Baseline (Where we are now)

Purpose: Establish a factual starting point before setting strategy.

Required elements:

Institutional status summary (size, scope, delivery modes)
Current course portfolio and status (active, new, phased out)
Student profile snapshot (numbers, mix, progression trends)
Staffing profile snapshot (FT/PT, academic/non-academic)
Key operational strengths
Key operational constraints
Current compliance status (SSG, ISO, ISMS, BizSafe if applicable)
Financial position at high level (stable, constrained, growth-capable)
Key risks currently active
Key performance outcomes from the previous cycle
Known capability gaps
Known system or process limitations
Output format:

Short narrative or structured table
Time-stamped for the planning cycle
