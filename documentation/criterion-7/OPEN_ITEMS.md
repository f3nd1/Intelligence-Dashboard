# Criterion 7 Open Items

The following metadata is required for complete ARTEMIA reporting:

- full Quality Performance Outcomes DocType definition;
- trend-history child table;
- benchmark source and validation child table;
- target-review and adjustment history;
- outcome-category controlled values;
- approved aggregation rules for institutional outcome rates;
- linkage from outcome gaps to Quality Action;
- definitions for any additional Criterion 7 policies not supplied.
