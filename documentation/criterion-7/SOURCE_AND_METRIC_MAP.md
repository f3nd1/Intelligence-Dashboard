# Criterion 7 Source and Metric Map

## Primary source

`Quality Performance Outcomes` is the policy-defined system of record for
outcomes, indicators, targets, benchmarks, trends and improvement monitoring.

## Supporting sources

- Quality Action
- Quality Meeting
- Management Review
- Operational Outcomes Cost Time Saving

## Live checks

- outcomes in scope;
- outcome-category completeness;
- KPI or indicator completeness;
- benchmark completeness;
- target completeness;
- actual-result completeness;
- target achieved;
- below target;
- measurement-date completeness;
- ownership completeness;
- open improvement actions;
- Quality Meetings and Management Reviews;
- operational net saving and benchmark variance.

Target achievement is calculated only when numeric actual and target fields
resolve on the installed DocType.
